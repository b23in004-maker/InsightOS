About InsightOS

Project Objective

InsightOS is a Decision Intelligence Platform designed to help organizations understand their existing data, identify unused information, detect missing business data, and evaluate business decisions using analytical insights.

Problem Statement

Businesses collect huge amounts of data but struggle to understand what data exists, what information is unused, what is missing, and whether strategic decisions actually improve organizational performance.
